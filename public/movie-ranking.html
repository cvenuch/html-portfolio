The best movies according to Venu

My top 3 movies all time.

OG

A high-octane gangster action drama starring Pawan Kalyan, OG explores the rise of a powerful don and his ruthless journey in the underworld.

Mirai

A heartwarming Japanese anime film, Mirai tells the story of a young boy who discovers love, family bonds, and self-growth through magical encounters with his future and past

Kantara

Blending folklore, divinity, and raw action, Kantara is a gripping tale rooted in coastal traditions that highlights the clash between man, nature, and faith.
